# Animal Sound Files

Add the following audio files to this directory for the animal sounds to work:

## Required Files:
- `lion.mp3` or `lion.wav` - Lion roar sound
- `elephant.mp3` or `elephant.wav` - Elephant trumpet sound
- `monkey.mp3` or `monkey.wav` - Monkey chatter sound
- `giraffe.mp3` or `giraffe.wav` - Giraffe hum sound
- `zebra.mp3` or `zebra.wav` - Zebra neigh sound
- `tiger.mp3` or `tiger.wav` - Tiger growl sound
- `dog.mp3` or `dog.wav` - Dog bark sound
- `cat.mp3` or `cat.wav` - Cat meow sound

## Audio Guidelines:
- Keep files small (under 500KB each)
- Use MP3 or WAV format
- Short clips (2-5 seconds) work best
- Clean, clear animal sounds without background noise

## Free Sound Resources:
- Freesound.org
- Zapsplat.com
- YouTube Audio Library
- Pixabay.com (audio section)

If audio files are not found, the site will automatically fall back to text-to-speech.
